Purpose Statement

This weather site aims to give week long forecasts including temperature, precipitation, winds and other conditions. This will be useful to adults planning travels and activities outdoors.

Audience Description

Audience around 18-60 years of age with high school education, single, married, or family with children. Some experience or new to weather sites. Using mobile devices or computers to see how weather will be affecting their schedule such as commuting or events outdoors.
